## Project Details

**Project Title:**
Notes Arena – Notes for All

**Project Description:**
Notes Arena is an open-source educational platform designed to help students easily access and share high-quality academic notes across various subjects and courses. The platform provides a simple, responsive, and user-friendly interface where learners can quickly find study materials without unnecessary complexity.

The primary goal of this project is to make educational resources more accessible and organized for students preparing for examinations and academic coursework. During the hackathon, the project was enhanced with improved UI, categorized note collections, PDF integration, and a streamlined browsing experience.
